About Us

Western Rideshare's goals is to provide quick and easy service in terms of finding rides to/from Western's campus. None of what we do is for profit it any capacity. All of the prices listed go directly to the driver with no portion coming to us in any way.

However, in order to run such a site there are fees required to host, maintain, and deploy our site. If any of you would like to donate, and help out this movement then check out our patreon page for more information! In addition, we also are looking for individuals with experience in web development to help out with running this site. If you are looking for a chance to develop, and be part of something big then don't hesitate to send us an email!
